Cervicogenic Headache Exercises

A Physiotherapy Guide

Important: Consult a physiotherapist before starting these exercises. They can assess your specific condition and ensure proper technique.

    General Tips

    Start slowly — Progress gradually, don't rush into full intensity.
    Pain rule — Never push through sharp pain. Stop if pain increases.
    Be consistent — Regularity matters more than intensity.
    Good posture — Maintain good posture throughout the day.
    Desk breaks — Stretch every 30-60 minutes at your desk.
    Ice / Heat — Heat before exercise, ice after for inflammation.
