Important: Compiled from physiotherapy literature and clinical protocols. Consult a physiotherapist before starting. Stop any exercise that worsens headache or causes sharp/radiating pain.

    General Tips

    Start slowly — Progress gradually, don't rush into full intensity.
    Pain rule — Never push through sharp pain. Stop if pain increases.
    Be consistent — Regularity matters more than intensity. Benefits appear within 4-6 weeks.
    Good posture — Maintain good posture throughout the day.
    Desk breaks — Shoulder blade squeezes + chin tucks every 1-2 hours.
    Full results — Full improvement may take 8-12 weeks of consistent daily practice.
